Getting Started

To commence, ensure that you undertake the following steps.

  1. Active Mobile Responsive
    Make sure to incorporate the <meta name="viewport"> tag for effective responsive functionality on mobile devices. 
                                                    
<!doctype html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="utf-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1">
    <title>LiveWire Page</title>
</head>
<body>
    <h1>Hello, world!</h1>
</body>
</html>
  2. Incorporate the Bootstrap JavaScript and CSS files.
    Position the tag within the section for our CSS, and the <script> tag for our JavaScript bundle just before the closing </body> tag. 
                                                                                        
<!--CSS -->
<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.2.2/dist/css/bootstrap.min.css" rel="stylesheet">

<!--JS -->
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.2.2/dist/js/bootstrap.bundle.min.js"></script>
  3. Familiarize yourself with the documentation
    Go through the documentation to acquaint yourself with the accessible UI elements

Typhography

Typography refers to the art and technique of arranging and designing text in a visually appealing and readable manner. It encompasses various elements such as font selection, font size, spacing, alignment, and formatting to create an aesthetically pleasing and legible written content.

Courgette

This is Courgette. Our brand typeface.

Courgette typography is a specific font or typeface characterized by its elegant and slightly whimsical appearance. It often features fluid, cursive-like letterforms with a unique personality, making it well-suited for decorative or creative design projects

Roboto is our supporting font.

Roboto typography is a modern and versatile typeface designed for digital interfaces and printed materials. It is known for its clean, neutral, and easily readable characters.

Font Scale

A font scale, in design and typography, refers to a consistent system of sizing fonts or typefaces relative to one another.
